Lesson 6: Common Vocabulary

In this lesson, you will expand your vocabulary with common words and phrases used in everyday conversations in Chadian Arabic.

Family Members

Here are some common words for family members in Chadian Arabic:

  • Father: أب (Ab)
  • Mother: أم (Um)
  • Brother: أخ (Akh)
  • Sister: أخت (Ukht)
  • Son: ابن (Ibn)
  • Daughter: ابنة (Ibna)
  • Husband: زوج (Zawj)
  • Wife: زوجة (Zawja)

Common Objects

Here are some common objects you might encounter in everyday life:

  • Book: كتاب (Kitab)
  • Table: طاولة (Tawila)
  • Chair: كرسي (Kursi)
  • Pen: قلم (Qalam)
  • Phone: هاتف (Hatf)
  • Computer: حاسوب (Hasub)
  • Car: سيارة (Sayara)
  • House: بيت (Bayt)

Food and Drinks

Here are some common words for food and drinks in Chadian Arabic:

  • Water: ماء (Ma')
  • Bread: خبز (Khubz)
  • Rice: أرز (Arz)
  • Meat: لحم (Lahm)
  • Chicken: دجاج (Dajaj)
  • Fish: سمك (Samak)
  • Fruit: فاكهة (Fakha)
  • Vegetables: خضروات (Khadrawat)

Practice Exercises

Practice using these common vocabulary words in sentences. Here are some exercises to help you get started:

  • Describe your family using the family member vocabulary.
  • List the objects you see in your room using the common objects vocabulary.
  • Describe your favorite meal using the food and drinks vocabulary.
  • Practice forming sentences with the new vocabulary words.

Practice these exercises regularly to become more comfortable with using common vocabulary in Chadian Arabic. In the next lesson, we will cover practice conversations to help you improve your speaking and listening skills.
